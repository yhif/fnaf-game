About FNAF 1

FNAF 1, short for Five Nights at Freddy's 1, is the original installment in the acclaimed indie horror series developed by Scott Cawthon. Set in the eerie environment of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, players assume the role of a night-time security guard tasked with surviving five terrifying nights. As midnight approaches, the animatronic characters—Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy—come to life, roaming the halls with sinister intentions. Utilizing limited resources like security cameras and door controls, players must strategically defend themselves against relentless jump scares. FNAF 1 captivates horror enthusiasts with its suspenseful gameplay, minimalist design, and immersive storyline, making it a must-play for fans of survival horror games.
