Game Overview

What is FNAF?

Five Nights at Freddy's (FNAF) is a survival horror game where you play as a night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Your task is to survive five nights while monitoring security cameras and managing limited power resources.

Key Features

  • Survive five terrifying nights
  • Monitor security cameras
  • Manage power resources
  • Face animatronic characters

How to Play

Check the Cameras

Monitor the security cameras to track the movement of animatronic characters throughout the restaurant.

Manage Power

Use your power wisely! Every action consumes electricity, and you need to make it last until 6 AM.

Close the Doors

When animatronics are near, close the security doors to protect yourself - but remember, this uses power!

Scoring System

Your score is based on several factors:

  • Surviving each night: +1000 points
  • Efficient power management: up to +500 points
  • Successfully tracking animatronics: +100 points each
  • Perfect night completion: +2000 bonus points

Tips and Tricks

Beginner Tips

  • Listen carefully for audio cues
  • Don't overuse the cameras
  • Keep track of Foxy in Pirate Cove
  • Only close doors when necessary

Advanced Strategies

  • Learn animatronic patterns
  • Develop a power conservation strategy
  • Master the camera switching technique
  • Time your door checks perfectly
