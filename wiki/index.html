Series Overview

Five Nights at Freddy's is a point-and-click survival horror game where players assume the role of Mike Schmidt, a night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. The objective is to survive from midnight to 6:00 AM while avoiding attacks from homicidal animatronics.

"At Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, the animatronics are programmed to entertain during the day, but at night they roam freely. As a night security guard, your job is to survive five nights while monitoring these potentially dangerous animatronics."

Core Concept

  • Players monitor security cameras and manage limited power resources
  • Each night lasts from 12 AM to 6 AM with increasing difficulty
  • Animatronics have unique AI behaviors and movement patterns
  • Power management is crucial for survival

Core Gameplay Mechanics

Power Management

The player's power supply starts at 100% and drains when:

  • Using security cameras
  • Closing doors
  • Turning on lights

Power consumption rates vary:

  • Doors consume more power than lights
  • Continuous camera usage steadily drains power
  • Power reaching zero results in system shutdown and Freddy's attack

Defensive Tools

  • Security cameras for monitoring animatronic movements
  • Two doors that can be closed to block animatronics
  • Lights to check for animatronics at doors
  • Ventilation system (in later games)

Animatronic AI and Movement System

The animatronic movement system operates on a 5-second interval check:

  • Each interval generates a random number (1-20)
  • Animatronics move if the number is less than their AI value
  • AI values increase as nights progress
  • Custom night allows setting AI values from 0 to 20

Night-by-Night Progression

Night 1

Basic introduction with minimal animatronic activity. Focus on learning camera and door mechanics.

Night 2

Increased animatronic movement. Power management becomes crucial.

Night 3

Foxy becomes active. More aggressive animatronic behavior.

Night 4

Maximum aggression. Phone Guy's message implies previous guard's death.

Night 5

Peak difficulty with fastest animatronic movements.

Custom Night

Player-set AI values. 20/20/20/20 mode requires perfect strategy.

Power Management Milestones

  • 1 AM: Aim for 80% power remaining
  • 2 AM: Maintain 60% power
  • 3 AM: Keep 40% power
  • 4 AM: Preserve 25% power
  • 5 AM: Survive with 9% power

Character Details and Behaviors

Freddy Fazbear

  • Leader of the animatronic band
  • Moves only when cameras are active
  • Special attack sequence when power dies
  • Laughs to signal movement

Bonnie the Bunny

  • Most aggressive animatronic
  • Frequent movements from left side
  • Can linger at doors
  • Distinctive footsteps sound

Chica the Chicken

  • Right-side approach pattern
  • Kitchen area specialist
  • Distinctive kitchen sounds
  • Moderate aggression level

Foxy the Pirate Fox

  • Unique hiding mechanic in Pirate Cove
  • Requires regular camera checks
  • Fast rush attack if neglected
  • Running sound effects

Story Elements and Lore

The Missing Children Incident

Five children were lured to a back room by William Afton (Purple Guy) wearing a Spring Bonnie suit. Their bodies were hidden in the animatronics, which became possessed by their spirits.

The Bite of '83/'87

Two critical incidents:

  • Bite of '83: Child bitten by Fredbear
  • Bite of '87: Employee bitten by animatronic
  • Resulted in removal of daytime walking capability

Animatronic Possession

  • Animatronics emit foul odors
  • Blood and mucus leaks reported
  • Mistake humans for endoskeletons
  • Attempt to stuff humans into suits
